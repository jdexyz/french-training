⚠️ Speech support not detected. For best results use Chrome, Edge or Safari.

Audio uses Google Translate's natural French voice by default (needs internet). Switch to any French voice on your computer with the Voice menu inside a level — your choice is remembered. Each round the spoken word is picked at random, so you have to listen — memorising answers won't help, and meanings stay hidden until you answer so you can't guess from sense. Tap the big button (or 🔊 Normal) to replay the prompt, 🐢 Slow to stretch it out, and the 🔊 / 🐢 on each card to hear that option fast or slow.
